Auditing

Qinxin accounting firm possesses its comparable advantages in audit service industry because of a group of professional and experienced experts recruited at home and abroad which ensures the nationally standardized audit service in parallel with international rules, combines optimum resources and coordinates with the taxation, audit and evaluation authorities for multidisciplinary services.

The qualifications for the audit of securities businesses and of central enterprises under the SASAC assure our services of annual audit and special audits for all listed companies, large government facilities, financial institutions, foreign-funded enterprises, excellent private enterprises, social associations, governmental organizations and public institutions, etc.

Our services include:

Audit of reorganization and listing of company

Investigation and evaluation of corporate internal control system

Audit of profit forecast

Annual audit, appraisal of properties and funds and debenture issue audit for state-owned enterprises

Audit of annual accounting statements

Annual audit and review of accounting statements for the foreign-funded enterprises

Financial and insurance audits

Audit of corporate acquisition, merge, recombination, liquidation, etc.

Financial investigation, audit of agreed procedures and other audit related businesses

Audit of economic responsibilities during the term of office

Audit of juridical authentication

Verification of registered capital and paid-up capital
