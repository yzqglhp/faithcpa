We own first-class project organization capabilities and ample evaluation experience, with the customers spreading in machinery, electronics, energy, pharmacy, chemical, textile, petroleum, power, communication, information technology, real estate, commerce, finance, insurance and the businesses covering all over the country.

  • Assets assessment for financial institutions (bank, securities, futures)
    • Evaluation on enterprise values (whole enterprise value, values of all or part of shareholder's equities ) concerned in economic activities such as acquisition and recombination (merge, purchase, transfer of stock rights, outward investments, loan disposition, etc.) of the financial institutions, partial (single) assets assessment

    • Assessments on fair values of financial assets and financial liabilities
